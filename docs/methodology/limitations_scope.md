# Limitations and Scope

The evidence is valid for the controlled AirSimNH/PX4 setup, the signaled landing platform, scenarios `S01` to `S08`, and the accepted formal runs.

The results do not claim validation for:

- Physical UAV flight.
- Other drones or camera placements.
- Natural unprepared terrain.
- Unseen lighting/weather conditions.
- A new ArUco detector.
- A universal flight-control law.
